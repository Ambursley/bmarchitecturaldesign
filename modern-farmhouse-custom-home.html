Modern Farmhouse Custom Home

Summary

This custom home was designed for a growing family on a large country property outside of Burlington.

Design Summary

The clients for this project loved the homey feeling of a country home and the curb appeal of modern home styles. For this reason it was decided to name this project the “Modern Farmhouse.” This project features very tall open spaces with vaulted ceilings and large windows. The opportunity for daylight in this home has been maximized and important for almost every living space. The exterior design features classic farmhouse textures contrasted with sleek clean lines and modern details.
