Burlington Custom Home

Summary

This large custom home is located on the outskirts of Burlington in a quiet country neighbourhood.

BM Architectural Design teamed up with another designer and took on the role of design and drafting to create the construction drawings and obtain a building permit.

This building has a complex design and structure with curved walls and has great curb appeal. Some other features include open social hubs spaces with unique and intriguing vertical space, overlooking library on the second floor and large covered rear deck including a fireplace feature for entertaining during warm seasons.
