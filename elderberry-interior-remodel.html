Elderberry Interior Renovations

Summary

BM Architectural Design worked with Frontiers Custom Renovations and Homes on this extensive interior remodel and renovation. This renovation resulted in a complete redesign of the main floor, basement and second floor resulting in major structural alterations to allow the openness of this stunning home.

Summary

These clients are a family who had a beautiful home and location, however they were not happy with the layout and flow of their house. It was important to create a layout that would meet their needs and allow them to keep their location while also having their dream home. This resulted in a complete remodel of the basement, main floor and second floor to create functional spaces that are open, airy and efficient.
