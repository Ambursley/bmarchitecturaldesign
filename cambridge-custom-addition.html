Cambridge Custom Addition

Summary

This family had outgrown their home but had a property in a neighbourhood that they did not want to leave. BM Architectural Design worked with this family to design an addition to their existing home that would give them the spaces and home they have always wanted.

Process

There was a few areas their existing home was lacking, they include - a Master Suite, Dining Room, Foyer Space, Storage and Connection to their beautiful backyard. This custom addition was designed to capitalize on these missing features of their existing home and to provide a seamless flow.

Features

The proposed design for this home includes a variety of features in upgrades including a new master suite with an outdoor oasis, dining room, open and airy flex space/breezeway, large covered porch and a two-car garage.
